Quarto Game

Rules of the Game

Quarto is a strategy board game played on a 4x4 grid. There are 16 unique pieces, each with four different attributes:

  • Color: black or white
  • Size: large or small
  • Shape: round or square
  • Symbol: (+) or (-)

Players take turns placing pieces on the board. However, you do not choose your own piece. Instead, your opponent selects which piece you must play.

The goal is to create a line of four pieces (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) that share at least one common attribute.

The player who completes such a line wins the game.
